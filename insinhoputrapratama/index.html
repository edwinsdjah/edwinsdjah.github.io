ABOUT US

PT Insinho is a private company in Indonesia which operates in the fields of ship repair, transportation, logistics, export and import of goods, and custom clearance.
Founded in 2012, PT Insinho has a lot of work experience and a logistics distribution network so that it provides the best solutions for every project.

Our Services

Ship Repair

Elevate vessel performance with our top-notch repair services, ensuring reliability on the high seas.

Transportation & Logistics

Experience efficient and reliable cargo movement tailored to your needs, delivering on time, every time.

Export & Import

Navigate global trade effortlessly with our expert guidance, ensuring smooth transactions and compliance.

Custom Clearance

Simplify the complexities of customs processes with our dedicated services, minimizing delays and ensuring regulatory compliance.
